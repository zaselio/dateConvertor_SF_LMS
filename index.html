Date Convertor for SAP-SF-LMS Connectors


Instructions:


1. Use the folowing date foramt: mm/dd/yyyy


2. Paste date then press "Add". The date will be add to the code.


3. You can add more dates, you'll have to split dates with space (ex. dd/mm/yyyy dd/mm/yyyy).


4. When you'r done press "Convert".


5. please be alert - when using bulk paste do validate your data after. You probably will have to perform servel bulk paste.
